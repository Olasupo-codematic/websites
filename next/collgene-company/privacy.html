Privacy Policy

Last updated:

COLLGENE COMPANY LIMITED ("COLLGENE", "we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect information when you use our website, make purchases, or interact with our digital products and services (collectively, the "Services").

Information We Collect

  • Contact details: such as name and email address when you contact us or complete a purchase.
  • Order information: product purchased, price, and transaction metadata from payment providers.
  • Usage data: pages visited, device/browser information, and general analytics.
  • Cookies and similar technologies: to remember preferences and improve the Services.

How We Use Information

  • To deliver purchased digital products and provide customer support.
  • To improve and personalize the Services and product content.
  • To send important communications such as receipts, updates, and security notices.
  • To protect the Services, prevent fraud, and enforce our Terms.

Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. We may share limited information with trusted service providers (e.g., hosting, analytics, and payment processors) under appropriate contracts, and with authorities when required by law or to protect rights and safety.

Payment Processing

We do not store sensitive card information on our systems. Payments are processed by third‑party providers according to their policies. We receive confirmation of payment status to fulfill your order and deliver your digital products.

Data Retention

We retain necessary customer and order records for as long as needed to provide Services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. Typically, order records are kept for up to five (5) years.

Security

We use reasonable and appropriate safeguards to protect information, including encryption in transit and access controls. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Your Rights and Choices

  • Request access to or deletion of your personal data, subject to applicable laws.
  • Update your information or preferences by contacting us.
  • Manage cookies through your browser settings.

Children’s Privacy

Our Services are not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us for removal.

International Transfers

Your information may be processed in countries other than your own. We take steps to ensure appropriate safeguards for such transfers, consistent with applicable law.

Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date reflects the most recent changes. Your continued use of the Services after changes indicates acceptance.

Contact Us

For questions or requests regarding this Privacy Policy, contact us at support@collgene.com.
