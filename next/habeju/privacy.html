Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal identification information only when you voluntarily provide it to us, primarily during the purchase and contact process. This data includes:

2. How We Use Your Data

The data collected is used strictly for the following purposes:

  • To fulfill your order and provide you with instant access to your digital files.

  • To respond to your inquiries via the contact form.
  • For internal record keeping and order tracking.

3. Data Security and Retention

HABEJU LIMITED is committed to ensuring that your information is secure. We use appropriate physical and electronic procedures to safeguard the information we collect online. We retain your purchase records and email address for as long as necessary to provide customer support or meet legal/accounting requirements.

4. Cookies and Tracking

This website uses minimal session cookies necessary for a smooth shopping experience (e.g., keeping track of a potential cart, though not fully implemented here). We do not use intrusive third-party tracking or advertising cookies.
