Privacy Policy

Last updated:

ELCHIC GENERAL WORKS LIMITED ("ELCHIC", "we", "us") respects your privacy. This Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect information when you use our website and purchase our digital products (the "Services").

Information We Collect

  • Contact details such as name and email when you reach out or complete a purchase.
  • Order details including product purchased, price, and transaction metadata from payment providers.
  • Usage data such as pages viewed, device/browser information, and basic analytics.
  • Cookies and similar technologies to remember preferences and improve the Services.

How We Use Information

  • To deliver digital products and provide customer support.
  • To improve and personalize the Services and product content.
  • To send receipts, updates, and important service communications.
  • To protect the Services, prevent fraud, and enforce our Terms.

Sharing and Transfers

We do not sell personal data. We may share limited information with service providers (hosting, analytics, payment processors) under contract, and with authorities when required by law. Your data may be processed outside your country with appropriate safeguards.

Payments

Payments are processed by secure third‑party providers. We do not store sensitive card details. We receive confirmation of payment status to fulfill your order and deliver your files.

Data Retention

We retain necessary customer and order records for as long as needed to provide the Services and meet legal obligations. Typically, order records are kept for up to five (5) years.

Security

We use reasonable safeguards to protect information, including encryption in transit and access controls. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Your Choices

  • Request access to or deletion of your data, subject to applicable laws.
  • Update your information or preferences by contacting us.
  • Manage cookies in your browser settings.

Children’s Privacy

The Services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date reflects the most recent changes.
