Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2025

Power Point Electricals Limited respects your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website or purchase services through our checkout provider.

Information We Collect

We may collect contact information (name, email, phone), service preferences, and payment data through our third-party checkout provider. We do not store raw payment card data on our servers — payment processing is handled by the checkout provider.

How We Use Information

We use collected information to fulfil service bookings, communicate about appointments, send invoices or receipts, and for support and legal compliance. With your consent we may send occasional service updates or marketing emails — you can opt out at any time.

Third Party Services

We work with third-party providers (payment processors, analytics, Firebase) who may access personal information as necessary to deliver their services. Their use of your information is governed by their privacy policies.

Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure. If you suspect a breach, contact us immediately.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to Hello@powerpointlimited.com.
