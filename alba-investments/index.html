Common Questions

How are the packages delivered?

All packages (content, strategy, reports, and calendars) are delivered securely via email within the specified turnaround time for your chosen package. This simple email delivery ensures fast deployment without complex client portals.

What if I need revisions?

Our packages include a single round of minor revisions on the content draft before final delivery. For complex or ongoing revision needs, we recommend the Scale or Custom packages which offer more hands-on consulting.

Do you handle the actual posting?

Our primary service is providing ready-to-use assets and strategies. We focus on Content Creation and Planning. However, we provide detailed scheduling instructions and can integrate with your existing scheduling tools like Buffer or Hootsuite.
