Privacy Policy

Last updated:

JAMUNET COMPANY LIMITED ("JAMUNET", "we", "us") respects your privacy. This Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you browse our website, purchase digital products, or contact support (the "Services").

Information We Collect

  • Contact details: name and email for communication and delivery.
  • Order details: product purchased, price, and payment status from our providers.
  • Usage data: pages viewed, device/browser, and general analytics signals.
  • Cookies: to remember preferences and improve the Services.

How We Use Information

  • Deliver digital products and provide customer support.
  • Improve and personalize product content and the overall experience.
  • Send receipts, updates, and important service communications.
  • Protect the Services, prevent fraud, and enforce our Terms.

Sharing and Transfers

We do not sell personal data. We may share limited information with service providers (hosting, analytics, payment processors) under contract, and with authorities when required by law. Your data may be processed outside your country with appropriate safeguards.

Payments

Payments are processed by secure third‑party providers. We do not store sensitive card details. We receive payment confirmation to fulfill orders and deliver files.

Data Retention

We retain necessary customer and order records for as long as needed to provide the Services and meet legal obligations. Typically, order records are kept for up to five (5) years.

Security

We use reasonable safeguards to protect information, including encryption in transit and access controls. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Your Choices

  • Request access to or deletion of your personal data, subject to applicable laws.
  • Update your information or preferences by contacting support.
  • Manage cookies in your browser settings.

Children’s Privacy

The Services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

We may update this Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date reflects the most recent changes.
